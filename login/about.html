About Our Disease Risk Predictor

APMEEX is a simple and powerful platform designed to help individuals understand their genetic and lifestyle-based risk of disease. Our form-based predictor takes into account various factors like genetic history, physical activity, sleep quality, and more.

Whether you're just curious or seriously tracking your health, our AI-powered prediction model helps you stay one step ahead. We're committed to data privacy, transparency, and supporting healthier lifestyles through modern technology.

Note: This tool is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.
